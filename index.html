Backpacking West

Backpacking West is a collection of trip reports/guides to Sam Grossberg’s favorite hiking destinations in the Western United States, with an emphasis on backpacking in the National Parks.

California

  • Yosemite: Tuolumne Meadows to Yosemite Village
  • Sequoia and Kings Canyon: Rae Lakes Loop

Colorado

  • Maroon Bells Wilderness: Four Pass Loop
  • Colorado Trail: Frenchman Creek Loop

Washington

  • Mt. Rainier: Wonderland Trail, Panhandle Gap
  • Enchantment Lakes Loop
